about me

Hi, I'm Sarah!

I'm an illustrator and freelance designer based in Northern Ireland. I've tried my hand at various areas of design, but my personal favourites but far are infographic design, illustration and web design.

WHAT I DO

I have a real passion for fun and personality-driven illustrations which tell a story. I especially have a penchant for the weird and wonderful.

DESIGN

I don't confine myself too strictly to one path, and love engaging with different projects that help me grow as a designer. Web design, data visualisation and UX are just a few that have really stuck.
